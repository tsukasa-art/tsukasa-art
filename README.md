# 月村つかさ (Tsukasa)

I care about the moments when something truly reaches someone.

Games, art, music, products, and people have shaped my life because they reached me at the right moment.

I believe good experiences deserve to reach the people who would love them.

When they don't, I want to understand why.

And once I understand it, I want to fix it.

## Selected Projects

### [Wukiyo](https://github.com/tsukasa-art/Wukiyo)

Making Windows-only visual novels accessible on Apple Silicon.

Wukiyo is a compatibility project for running Japanese visual-novel games on Apple Silicon Mac, with a launcher, per-title environments, and an engine compatibility database.

[Website](https://wukiyo.app) · [Compatibility DB](https://wukiyo.app/compat) · [Zenn series](https://zenn.dev/tsukasa_art/articles/mac-eroge-compat-part1)

### [zenpix](https://github.com/tsukasa-art/zenpix)

Image processing built for constrained infrastructure and real-world deployment.

zenpix is a C image processing engine with resizing and AVIF/WebP encoding, designed to avoid unnecessary CPU saturation on small VPS environments.

[npm](https://www.npmjs.com/package/zenpix) · [Docs](https://zenpix.tsukasa-art.com)
